My Goat - Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is my GOAT for various reasons. At club level he has won everything there is to win at the highest level of futbol. At international level he was ridiculed for not winning any major international trophies such as the World Cup and the Copa America. However, that all changed when in 2021 he won the Copa America with Argentina. A year later he achieved his dream winning the World Cup which resulted in mainstream recognition of his GOAT status in the game. But more than the statistics and trophies he's won, the way he plays the game, the way he caresses and moves with the ball, is unique unlike something I've ever seen. In my eyes he is a total futbol player, he can do almost everything in the game.
